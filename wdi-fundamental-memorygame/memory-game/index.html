Instructions

Concentration, also know as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijak, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over paris of matchings cards.
